# Extended Graph - Huong dan

## Cach su dung Extended Graph

1. Mo **Graph View** (Ctrl+G)
2. Panel ben trai se co them cac bo loc moi tu Extended Graph:
   - **Filter by Tags**: Loc theo tag (contact, company, project...)
   - **Filter by Properties**: Loc theo company, role...
   - **Focus Node**: Click vao 1 node de highlight connections
   - **Pin Node**: Ghim node quan trong

## Recommended Filters

### Chi xem Contacts + Companies
- Enable tags: `contact`, `company`
- Disable: `project`, `meeting`

### Chi xem Project Stakeholders
- Enable tags: `project`, `company`, `maincontractor`, `consultant`, `developer`
- Disable: `contact` (qua nhieu nodes)

### Tim quan he giua 2 entities
- Focus Node: click vao entity A
- Tim duong noi den entity B trong graph
